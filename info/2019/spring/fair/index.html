CS 125 Spring 2019 Project Fair

The Third Biannual CS 125 Project Fair will take place Thursday 5/2/2019 1 from 1–3PM in Siebel. 147 groups will exhibit their Android apps. An award ceremony will follow on Friday 5/3/2019 at 1:30PM in Lincoln Hall Auditorium.

294 CS 125 students will be demoing lots of cool Android projects, so please come by to check them out!

Will there be food? You'll have to come and find out. But definitely bring your own beverage.

  • What: CS 125 Final Project Fair
  • When: Thursday 5/2/2019, judging from 1–3PM in Siebel and awards on Friday 5/3/2019 at 1:30PM in Lincoln Hall Auditorium
  • Where: Demos throughout Siebel, awards on Friday at 1:30PM in Lincoln
