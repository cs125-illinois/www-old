CS 125 Fall 2018 Project Fair

The Second Biannual CS 125 Project Fair will take place Thursday 12/13/2018 1 from 5–8PM in Siebel. 247 groups will exhibit their Android apps. An award ceremony will follow at 8PM in Foellinger Auditorium.

494 CS 125 students will be demoing lots of cool Android projects, so please come by to check them out!

Will there be food? You'll have to come and find out. But definitely bring your own beverage.

  • What: CS 125 Final Project Fair
  • When: Thursday 12/13/2018, judging from 5–8PM in Siebel and awards at 8PM in Foellinger
  • Where: Demos throughout Siebel, awards at 8PM in Foellinger
