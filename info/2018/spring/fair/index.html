CS 125 Spring 2018 Project Fair

The First Biannual CS 125 Project Fair will take place Thursday 5/3/2018 1 from 4–6PM in Siebel. 165 groups will exhibit their Android apps.

494 CS 125 students will be demoing lots of cool Android projects, so please come by to check them out!

Will there be food? You'll have to come and find out. But definitely bring your own beverage.

  • What: CS 125 Final Project Fair
  • When: Thursday 5/3/2018 from 4–6PM, with awards at 5:30PM in Siebel 1404
  • Where: Siebel Center ground floor and basement
