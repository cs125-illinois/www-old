CS 125 Fall 2019 Project Fair

The Fourth Biannual CS 125 Project Fair will take place Thursday 12/12/2019 1 from 4–6PM in Siebel. 248 groups will exhibit their Android apps.

496 CS 125 students will be demoing lots of cool Android projects, so please come by to check them out!

Will there be food? You'll have to come and find out. But definitely bring your own beverage.

  • What: CS 125 Final Project Fair
  • When: Thursday 12/12/2019, judging from 4–6PM in Siebel
  • Where: Demos throughout Siebel

Competition Winners

These four projects were deemed the most impressive by our judges. Two are by groups of students that were beginners when they started CS 125—although who are clearly no longer beginners.

First Place: Office Hour 125
First Place: Word Search Solver 
Second Place: Challenmander
Second Place: Pokemon 125
