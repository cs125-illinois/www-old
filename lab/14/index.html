Final Project Submission

Today is our last "lab" for this semester—but all you really need to do is submit your final project. As a reminder, your lab staff will choose two projects to receive extra credit.

1. Final Project Submission

Please use this form to submit information about your project: a link to your YouTube video, a link to your GitHub repository, and a team name for or online fair page.

1.1. Most Impressive Projects

Your TA and lab course staff will choose two most impressive projects: one done by a team of beginners, and a second done by anyone. These groups will receive 1% extra credit.
