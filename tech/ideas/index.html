CS 125 Development Ideas

Want to help improve the most innovative course on campus?

We always have a variety of exciting projects involving lots of different technologies and environments. Peruse the full list here. But it’s safe to say that if you like building things that hundreds of people will use, we probably have a project that will interest and challenge you.

Currently we’re working on things like

  1. making enormous lectures fun and interactive,

  2. new tools allowing us to embed questions anywhere,

  3. frameworks enabling crowdsourced code review at scale,

  4. bringing data structures to life through intelligent interactive visualizations,

  5. monitoring how students and course staff interact with the class through new apps and IntelliJ plugins,

  6. continuing to improve our existing MPs, including more use of Android,

  7. and a variety of other interesting and cool projects.

For more details about these projects, or if you have ideas or comments, feel free to contribute to our brainstorming document.
